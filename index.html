About Me

Where Clinical Care Meets Administrative Excellence

I am Gabrielle Eze — a certified Medical Virtual Assistant and experienced Healthcare Assistant with over three years of hands-on clinical experience in hospital and home-based care settings.

My background spans patient care at Elegbeleye Specialist Hospital in Lagos, remote care coordination with Ontario Health atHome teams in Canada, and executive administration supporting C-suite leaders across corporate and government sectors.

As a Medical VA, I bring both the clinical understanding of a trained healthcare professional and the administrative precision of a seasoned executive assistant — a combination that allows me to serve medical practices with exceptional accuracy, empathy and efficiency.
